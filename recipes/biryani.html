Biryani Recipe-

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is made with fragrant basmati rice, tender meat (such as chicken, mutton, or beef), and a blend of spices that create a rich and delicious flavor. Biryani is often garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and sometimes boiled eggs or nuts. It is a popular dish enjoyed by people of all ages and is often served during special occasions and celebrations.

Ingredients-

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps-

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and set it aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the sliced onions. Cook until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are well-cooked.
  5. Add all the spices and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the rinsed rice and mix gently.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fried onions, fresh herbs, and boiled eggs or nut and it's ready to be served.
